Ethiopian Restaurants

Ethiopia has a very diverse culture, and thus a wide variety of culture themed restaurants can be found.
There are five star, world renowned restaurants that offer a lavish experience as well as more affordable options that still have that Ethiopian vibe. Listing all or even half of the hotels in Ethiopia would be impractical for us and confusing for you.
Listed below are just some of the most amazing restaurants in Ethiopia, specifically in Addis Ababa, the capital of the nation. The selection is based on stars, user reviews and also on whether they offer the native, traditional dishes of the Ethiopian Cuisine.
If you are a tourist in Ethiopia or a native looking for a restaurant that offers Ethiopian dishes, this selection should be more than enough.
