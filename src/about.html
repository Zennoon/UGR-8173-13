About Us

Our Story

The Ethiopian Cuisine team is made up of Addis Ababa University Students who come from different backgrounds and different cultures, but united
in the idea that Ethiopian culture is worth sharing and worth experiencing.

Our Motive

Ethiopia is one of the most diverse countries in the world in terms of culture and practices.
With this diversity comes the vast amount of different culinary options throughout the country with different tastes.
However, our team has observed that most of these cultural foods of Ethiopia are overlooked or not widely known due to the lack of awareness.
This is what motivated us to organize our resources to create a platform that will share the amazing dishes available in the Ethiopian cuisine.

Our Mission

The goal of this website is to eventually make the Ethiopian cuisine widely known in the world
and place it in tis rightful place among the best cuisines in the world.
