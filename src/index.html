Home

Ethiopian Cuisine is a website, the first of its kind in Ethiopia, dedicated to enlighten the world on the deliquecies of the Ethiopian Dish. The Ethiopian cuisine has some of the most flavorfull and seasoned dishes in the world that will leave your taste buds caving for more. The large variety of spices and herbs in Ethiopia make the dishes amazing to the taste.

However, the deficiency of resources for marketing and unavailability of online platforms makes it so that this amazing cuisine is not widely known in other parts of the world.

This website aims at providing ample information about the Ethiopian cuisine including some of the spices, recipes to the most famous dishes and most suggested restaurants in Ethiopia. So, whether you are a tourist looking for the top restaurants in Ethiopia or just a person (Ethiopian or not) looking to make the dishes yourself at home, this website is for you
